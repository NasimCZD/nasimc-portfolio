Meet the Author

Nasim Choroumi

Nasim Choroumi is a seasoned Technical Program Manager with a decade of experience leading complex, cross-functional initiatives across technology, cybersecurity, and product development. With a Bachelor in Computing and Information Systems from Goldsmiths College, University of London, and a Master of Science in Project Management from Boston University, she brings a unique fusion of hands-on engineering knowledge, systems thinking, and a passion for emerging technologies. As an educator and Mentor, Nasim has taught project management at college level and guided early career professionals through the challenges of learning and leading in technical fields. She believes that the best way to understand technology is to build it - experimenting, troubleshooting, and learning through real-world experience.

How to Build a Line Follower Robot Using Barcodes grew out of Nasim's own early experiments in circuit design, microcontroller programming, and robotics. In her publications, she blends technical instructions with stories of persistence and discovery, aiming to inspire the next generation of builders, thinkers, and innovators.

Her upcoming research targets the convergence of edge computing and ecological responsibility—demonstrating that tomorrow's smartest autonomous agents do not require expensive equipment or massive carbon footprints.
